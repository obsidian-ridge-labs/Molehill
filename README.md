## What is Molehill

Molehill is built for ADHD and for anyone who has ever opened a to-do list and shut down. Instead of showing you the whole intimidating pile, it breaks a task into small, concrete, verb-first steps and shows you only the next one. The AI runs entirely on your device using Apple's Foundation Models, so your brain dumps never leave your phone and it works in airplane mode.

## How it works

1. **Dump it.** Type the thing stressing you out, or pour out everything on your mind at once.
2. **Break it down.** On-device AI turns it into small, concrete steps you can actually start, at the granularity you choose.
3. **Do one step.** Focus mode and the Lock Screen widget show just the next step. Begin there.
4. **Keep moving.** Finish a step, advance to the next. No streaks to break, no guilt if you pause.

## Features

- **One step at a time** in a full-screen Focus mode, with an optional gentle timer (5, 10, 15, or 25 minutes)
- **Brain dump** capture that sorts into six life areas, by hand for free or with AI
- **Granularity control**: Big Picture, Balanced, or Every Tiny Step
- **Next Step widget** for the Lock Screen and Home Screen
- **Dynamic Island Live Activity** with a live focus timer
- **Siri and Shortcuts** support, plus a Share Extension for quick capture
- **Honest AI fallback**: when Apple Intelligence is unavailable, a built-in heuristic still breaks tasks down
- **No streaks, no shame**, gentle by design

## Privacy

Molehill collects nothing. There is no account, no server, and no analytics or tracking of any kind. Every bit of AI runs on your device, and your tasks never leave your iPhone. It works fully in airplane mode. Read the full policy at https://obsidianridgelabs.com/privacy.
